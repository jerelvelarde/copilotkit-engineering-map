# Examples - v1 chat-with-your-data

**Framework:** Next.js 16 (React 19) workspace example (`@copilotkit-examples/chat-with-your-data`), `workspace:*` deps. Uses Tremor charts + `react-hook-form`.

**Demonstrates:** An AI-powered **dashboard assistant** — ask questions about your data in natural language, get insights, and interact with metrics through a conversational interface. Exercises the V1 readable-context + frontend-action surface ([[react-core - useCopilotReadable (v1)]], [[react-core - V1 hooks (useCopilotAction/useCoAgent/…)]]).

**CopilotKit packages:** [[@copilotkit/react-core]], [[@copilotkit/react-ui]], [[@copilotkit/runtime]] (`workspace:*`).

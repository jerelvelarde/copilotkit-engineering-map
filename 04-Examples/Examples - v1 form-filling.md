# Examples - v1 form-filling

**Framework:** Next.js 15 (React 19) workspace example (`@copilotkit-examples/form-filling`), `workspace:*` deps. `react-hook-form` + Radix inputs.

**Demonstrates:** A **form-filling copilot** that turns field-by-field data entry into a conversation — the assistant asks the right questions, understands context, and completes forms for the user. A clean illustration of V1 frontend actions ([[react-core - V1 hooks (useCopilotAction/useCoAgent/…)]]) reading/writing form state.

**CopilotKit packages:** [[@copilotkit/react-core]], [[@copilotkit/react-ui]], [[@copilotkit/runtime]] (`workspace:*`).
